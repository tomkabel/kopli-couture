Our Story

A journey through time, fashion, and culture

The Beginning

Kopli Couture was born from a passion for preserving the authentic spirit of Soviet-era fashion while creating a space where art and community thrive. Founded in 2020, our venue has become a beloved gathering place in Tallinn's historic Kopli district.

What started as a small vintage clothing collection has blossomed into something much more special - a dual-purpose space that honors the past while embracing the present.

By Day: Vintage Fashion

During daylight hours, Kopli Couture operates as a carefully curated vintage clothing shop. Each piece in our collection tells a story of Soviet-era craftsmanship and design. From authentic military jackets to elegant dresses from the 1960s-80s, we source our items from estates, personal collections, and vintage markets across Eastern Europe.

Our knowledgeable staff can help you understand the history behind each garment, making your shopping experience both educational and inspiring. Whether you're a fashion historian, a costume designer, or simply someone who appreciates quality vintage pieces, you'll find treasures here.

By Night: Cocktail Lounge

As evening descends, Kopli Couture transforms into an intimate cocktail bar. The clothing racks are lit with warm amber lighting, creating a unique atmosphere where you can sip classic cocktails surrounded by vintage elegance.

Our drinks menu features both timeless classics and our own creative interpretations, each crafted with attention to detail that matches our approach to fashion. The transformation from shop to bar happens seamlessly, creating a magical evening ambiance.

Community & Culture

Beyond fashion and drinks, Kopli Couture serves as a cultural hub for local artists, musicians, and vintage enthusiasts. We host monthly art exhibitions, live music performances, vintage fashion workshops, and themed events that bring our community together.

We believe in supporting local talent and preserving cultural heritage. Our space is available for private events, creative collaborations, and community gatherings that align with our values.
